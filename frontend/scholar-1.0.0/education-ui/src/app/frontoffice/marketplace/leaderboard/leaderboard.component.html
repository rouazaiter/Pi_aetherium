How Score Is Calculated

Final Score = 0.85 x Real Score + 0.15 x AI Quality

Real performance remains the primary ranking factor. AI quality improves ranking precision without dominating business outcomes.

Real Score (85%)

  • Success rate on applications.
  • Rejection rate penalty.
  • Engagement volume normalization.
  • Request closure rate.
  • Expiry rate penalty.
  • Request attractiveness and activity volume.

AI Quality (15%)

  • Relevance: 40%.
  • Clarity: 30%.
  • Structure: 20%.
  • Professionalism: 10%.
